Residential Lease Agreement Generator

Comprehensive South African residential lease for two independent tenants renting separate rooms / portions of one residential property.

Purpose: This single-file HTML tool captures lease information, generates a long-form lease, includes tenant-specific schedules and annexures, and supports print/PDF/email-summary workflows. It is a strong operational template, but it should still be reviewed by a South African attorney before signature and use in a dispute.
1. Owner / Lessor Details
A physical South African service address should be captured. Email can be added as an additional notice channel.
2. Banking and Payment Details
3. Premises and Occupation
4. Lease Period, Renewal and Cancellation
5. Tenant 1 Details
6. Tenant 2 Details
7. Utilities, Municipal Charges and Services
8. House Rules
9. Entry Condition and Inspection Notes
10. Digital Signatures

Use a mouse, trackpad, stylus, or touch screen. Signatures are embedded into the generated lease PDF. For stronger evidence, also retain emails/WhatsApp acceptance, IDs, proof of payment and a signed PDF copy.
